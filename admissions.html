    Admissions

    We’ve simplified the process and made it easy for you to enroll your little one at MSH

    Just Follow These Simple Steps

    Pre-Appliction

    Manhattan Schoolhouse offers rolling admissions. The first step is placing your name on the waitlist. Please download the application below and mail it in to begin the process. Once we receive the pre-application form, you will receive an invoice via email for the $100 non-refundable application fee, which can be paid online to secure a spot on the waitlist. At the same time, we encourage you to book a tour online. Please email us with any questions.

    Pre-Appliction

    Schedule A Tour

    You can book a tour by clicking on the “Book a Tour” option on the right side of the menu. (TBD) During tours, families will be able to learn more about our programs, philosophy, curriculum, and ask questions.

    Book A Tour

    Official Application

    Once a spot becomes available, the admissions director will contact you. From there, you will be asked to submit the online application, which is accessed through a password. After you submit the online application, we will contact you to schedule a home visit.

    Fill Out The Online Application

    Home Visit

    Home visits are conducted by the admissions director and education director from the program to which you have applied. Home visits provide an opportunity for potential families to learn more about the philosophy, policies, and procedures of the school. Questions that were not answered during a tour and more personal questions can be addressed during the home visit

    Enrollment

    Once a family receives the official invitation to join MSH, they will receive a contract and enrollment package.

    • First invoice; First month, one month refundable deposit + annual supplies fee
    • Medical form which includes vaccination and immunization records
    • Authorized pick up form Photo Consent form
    • Emergency contact form
    • Waiver of participation

    Children are not able to begin at Manhattan Schoolhouse until the Department of Health requirements have been met.

    Registration Requirements

    Before admission can be finalized, parents/guardians must submit the following documents:

    NYC Department of Health Day Care Cumulative Health Record
    NYC Department of Health Day Care Cumulative Health Record
    NYC Department of Health Child Health Examination Form
    Parents/guardians must ensure all NYC Department of Health requirements have been met.
