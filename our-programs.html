    Daycare

    Infants 3-12 months and Toddlers 12 -24 months

    Infants, Toddlers and Two Year Olds.

    Manhattan Schoolhouse daycare provides a Reggio Emilia inspired environment in which our youngest children develop loving relationships with their teachers and peers.

    Learning Values

    Children are taught to respect themselves and others, learn from one another, and express their needs and ideas kindly

    Building Trust

    As the children grow into preschoolers, They learn to collaborate with each other and express their understanding of the world. In every room of the daycare, children are provided with abundant opportunities to study natural materials and their environment.

    Outdoor Fun

    All children participate in weekly specials. Enrichment classes include music, yoga/infant massage, dance, soccer or gym, and a language. The children enjoy outdoor time throughout the year. Our infants use strollers, while the older children take neighborhood walks and play in nearby park

    Healthy Snacks

    Manhattan Schoolhouse serves nutritious breakfast, lunch, and snacks to the children starting in the toddler program. Parents of children in the infant rooms provide all meals until the children transition to the next program

    All policies and procedures are guided by the Department of Health regulations for early childhood programs

    Pre School

    3-K and Pre-K

    3-K Three Year Olds and Pre-K Four Year Olds.

    Manhattan Schoolhouse partners with the New York City Department of Education to offer 3-K. Our preschool classes follow Reggio Emilia inspired learning techniques, which align with the NYC Department of Education curriculum.

    Curriculum

    The 3-K and Pre-K programs at Manhattan Schoolhouse follow Reggio Emilia inspired learning, which align with the NYC Department of Education curriculum.

    Enrichment Classes

    Enrichment classes include music, dance, soccer, language and chess. The children take daily walks throughout the neighborhood and play in the local parks.

    Healthy Snacks

    Manhattan Schoolhouse offers breakfast and serves nutritious lunch and snacks to the children. The 3-K day at Manhattan Schoolhouse begins at 8:30 a.m. and ends at 2:50 p.m.

    All policies and procedures are guided by the Department of Health regulations for early childhood programs

    Wrap Around

    3-K and Pre-K

    3-K Three Year Olds and Pre-K Four Year Olds.

    Manhattan Schoolhouse offers year-long extended day programs for 3-K and Pre-K children. MSH remains open before and after school, during holidays when public schools are closed and throughout the summer.

    Daily Enrichment Classes

    By enrolling in the year-long wrap around program, families are welcome to drop off their children before the 3-K day, starting at 8 a.m., and can pick them up by 6 p.m. Children in the wrap around program enjoy daily afternoon enrichment classes, which include science and engineering, music, art, outdoor sports, dance, and language.

    Healthy Snacks

    Additionally, children are provided with an afternoon snack and take walks throughout the neighborhood.

    Summer Camp

    Summer camp is included in the 12-month program. Campers participate in swim lessons and soccer classes throughout the week.

    All policies and procedures are guided by the Department of Health regulations for early childhood programs
